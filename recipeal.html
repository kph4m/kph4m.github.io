Overview

Recipeal is a recipe manager that allows users to easily store and organize their favorite recipes, and help them explore new ones.

Problem

Busy people and cooking novices need a way to find and organize recipes easily in order to save time looking and preparing for meals.

Background

With how busy our lives have become due to work and family obligations, it can be difficult to make time to find the right recipes to make and organize them so theyâ€™re easily accessible. People need a centralized and convenient place to find new recipes and organize them so that theyâ€™re easily accessible. Although there are many recipe managers out there that could help with this, many of them have a confusing user interface and too many extra features, which can clutter the interface and make it hard to navigate. To address these problems, my team created Recipeal, a recipe manager that allows users to easily store and organize their favorite recipes, and help them explore new ones. We focused on making the design simple and easy-to-use so that users can solely focus on accomplishing their cooking tasks without the unnecessary clutter and confusing navigation.
