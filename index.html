About

     I'm a fourth-year Cognitive Science: Design and Interaction major with a Computer Science and Design minor at the University of California, San Diego. The multi-disciplinary nature of my major has exposed me to a wide variety of fields, such as web development, human-computer interaction, data science, design, and ui/ux. Currently, I have a strong interest in web and software development, and ui/ux.


      I have experience creating web applications through my courses, personal projects, and hackathons that I've participated in. Additionally, I have experience in design and ui/ux from the various projects I've done, where I got familiar with design concepts such as research, ideation, prototyping, and testing.


     When I'm not working on side projects, I love learning new skills and technologies, and incorporating them into future projects. I also like to dabble in computer peripheral customization.
